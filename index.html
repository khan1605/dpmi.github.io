    About Us

    DPMI MADHUBANI

    Delhi Paramedical & Management Institute, popularly known as DPMI in the Education sector was established in Delhi with the objective of providing quality Paramedical and Technical Management education for under 10th, 10+2, graduate and postgraduate levels.

    DPMI Madhubani is focused on value addition to the courses that it offers to the student community and provides the unique combination of technical excellence, opportunities, choices and experience. With rapid globalization of all economic activities, it is imperative that students develop a global outlook to become successful in the International arena.
